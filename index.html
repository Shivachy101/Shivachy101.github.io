Some important things you want to include in your webpage

  • Including a title
  • Including a body
  • Including !doctype htm
  • There are many more these are just a few

Why one wants to include a title on their web page: The title is crucial in helping search engines understand the page’s topic and relevance. It also influences how the page is displayed in search results and affects user engagement.

Why one wants to include a body on their web page:The body tag holds the “meat” of your web page, and will include your page’s content. It holds all of your div, img, p, etc tags. Commonly, people also insert styles and stylesheet or javascript references in the body instead of the head.

Why one wants to include !doctype htm in their web page: The doctype tag should always be placed at the beginning of the source code of HTML documents and sub-pages. Thanks to DOCTYPE, you can tell at first glance what type of document you’re dealing with. If the declaration is missing in the document, it can lead to errors in how your website is displayed.

Some info about headers

The importance of headers: HTML headers play a pivotal role in structuring web content and enhancing its accessibility. They not only provide a hierarchy to your content but also improve the overall user experience.

Why validate your code ?

Here is one reason , W3C validation is the process of checking a website's code to determine if it follows the formatting standards. If you fail to validate your website's pages based on W3C standards, your website will most likely suffer from errors or poor traffic owing to poor formatting and readability.
